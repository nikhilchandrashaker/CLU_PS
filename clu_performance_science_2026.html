About This Project

This performance science analysis integrates physical, physiological, and biomechanical datasets from the Cal Lutheran Kingsmen (Baseball) and Regals (Softball) Spring 2026 seasons. It demonstrates applied sports analytics methodology aligned with professional performance science workflows.

Physical datasetsExit velocity, sprint speed, barrel rate (Trackman/HitTrax analogs)
Biomechanical dataSpin rate, arm slot, pitch movement profiles (Trackman/Kinetrax analogs)
Physiological monitoringHRV RMSSD, session RPE, sleep tracking, ACWR (WHOOP/Polar analogs)
MethodswOBA, FIP, BABIP, K-Means clustering, PCA, ACWR (Foster 1998)
PlayersReal 2026 CLU roster names (clusports.com)
Physical metricsSimulated at realistic NCAA DIII benchmarks

References: Foster (1998) session-RPE; Gabbett (2016) ACWR; Tango, Lichtman & Dolphin (2006) wOBA.
